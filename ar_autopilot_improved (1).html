Accounts Receivable — Fast Track Prototype

AR Autopilot

A self-contained demo that turns a manual AR workflow into an operating system: age invoices, prioritize follow-up, draft reminders, reconcile payments, close invoices, and log exceptions for human review.

Manual work it replaces

Before: download invoices, calculate aging, decide who to chase, write emails, compare bank lines, update a spreadsheet. After: one AR cycle creates the work queue and closes matches.

Healthcare-safe demo data

Uses provider/customer invoices only. No patient names, diagnoses, clinical notes, or PHI. In production, payments and reminders would sit behind role-based access and audit logs.

Production path

Connect billing system + bank feed + secure server-side LLM endpoint. Keep humans in the loop before send, especially for high-risk or disputed accounts.
